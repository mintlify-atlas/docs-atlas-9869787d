## What is Airbyte?

Airbyte is an open-source data movement platform that makes it easy to replicate data from APIs, databases, files, and more into your data warehouse, data lake, or any other destination. With 300+ pre-built connectors, a powerful public API, and a visual UI, Airbyte is the fastest way to move data reliably at scale.

## How Airbyte works

Airbyte moves data in three steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Extract">
    Airbyte reads data from your **source** (a database, API, file, etc.) using a source connector. The connector authenticates, discovers the available data streams, and reads records incrementally or in full refresh mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load">
    Records are written to your **destination** (a data warehouse, database, or file storage) using a destination connector. Airbyte handles schema creation, type coercion, and deduplication.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transform (optional)">
    After loading, you can run dbt transformations on your destination to normalize or model your data. Airbyte integrates natively with dbt Cloud and dbt Core.
  </Step>
</Steps>
